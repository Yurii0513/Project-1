Delamar Academy London, Creating the best make-up careers since 1986

 
Delamar Academy London has been established for over 35 years as the most highly regarded school in the UK for make-up and hair courses.  We are the only British school to have trained Oscar, BAFTA and Emmy award winning artists, as well as a Vogue Make-up Artist of the year and an Indian Academy Award.  We take great pride in placing many of our graduates on top films, TV productions, theatre shows and fashion shoots. Delamar Academy tutors represent the leading professionals in the industry for their specific skill-sets.
The industry is extremely buoyant and a huge number of our graduates are out working on films, TV dramas, fashion shoots, music videos, commercials, and on theatre and opera productions. Our Instagram @delamaracademy has more details of our student and graduate success stories. If you are considering a career in the industry, please contact us to schedule an appointment to discuss your options.
